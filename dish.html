How to make Tamarind Rice

TAMARIND RICE

INGREDIENTS

  1. Rice
  2. Tamarind
  3. Chana Dal
  4. Urad Dal
  5. Green Chillies
  6. Red chillies
  7. Lemon Juice
  8. Salt
  9. Oil
  10. Turmeric Powder
  11. Curry Leaves

PROCEDURE TO MAKE

Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add chana dal, urad dal, and red chilies. Saute until the dals are golden brown. Add curry leaves and saute for a few seconds. Add tamarind extract or lemon juice and bring to a simmer. Add cooked rice to the pan and mix well with the tamarind mixture. Season with salt to taste. Garnish with chopped cilantro and grated coconut (if using). Serve hot or at room temperature.
